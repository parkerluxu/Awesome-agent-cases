# AI Agent Scientific Research Automation Cases

> A toolkit of AI agents for researchers: automate literature review, data analysis, experiment design, and paper writing.

This page is the scientific-research chapter of *Awesome-agent-cases*. It collects actionable tutorials, open-source projects, papers, and tools. For daily breaking news, see the AI News Bot digest in `daily_news/`.

## Scope

- **Literature review & knowledge synthesis**: agents that search, read, and summarize papers (review, citation graphs, survey generation)
- **Data analysis & scientific computing**: agent workflows over research data (statistics, modeling, visualization, reproducible experiments)
- **Experiment / lab automation**: experiment design, simulation, lab notebooks, and hyperparameter search
- **Paper writing & submission**: drafting, polishing, figures, formatting, and submission support
- **The "AI Scientist" paradigm**: end-to-end autonomous research (hypothesis, execution, reflection)

## How to use

The "Resource Update" blocks below are appended automatically by the script from search, RSS, GitHub, and arXiv, grouped by content type (Projects / Papers / Tutorials / Products) as newly discovered leads for this topic.
