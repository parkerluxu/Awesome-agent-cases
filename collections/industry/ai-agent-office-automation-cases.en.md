# AI Agent Office Automation Cases

> Hand everyday office and personal productivity to agents: email, documents, scheduling, meeting notes, plus notes, knowledge management, and learning workflows.

This page is the office-automation chapter of *Awesome-agent-cases*, covering actionable tutorials, open-source projects, products, and tools for "daily work and life". For daily news, see the AI News Bot digest in `daily_news/`.

## Scope

- **Office workflow automation**: email, docs, spreadsheets, scheduling, and meeting notes across apps
- **RPA + agents**: cross-app automation and orchestration (n8n, Zapier, Make, ...)
- **Personal productivity & knowledge management**: second brain, note automation, learning agents, inbox zero
- **Productivity-suite AI**: Copilot, Google Workspace AI, Notion AI, and other embedded agents
